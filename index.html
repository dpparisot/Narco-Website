New American Reel Company LLC.

New American Reel Company LLC., was founded to provide wire reel repair services for reels, bobbins and spools used in the manufacturing or shipping of wire products. Over the years our capabilities have expanded, and now include welding, machining, and the design and fabrication of new wire reels or drums.

  • Nailed Wooden Reels

    Now available from New American Reel Company, nailed wooden reels made to order. Using Heat Treated softwoods ranging from Southern Yellow Pine to Spruce, Pine, or Fir, we manufacture reels applying Lean concepts to provide outstanding quality at competitive prices.
